Thrifting

My third and final hobby is my newest one, thrifting. I absolutely love thrifting and finding super cool and unique things that I wouldn't be able to find anywhere else. It's made me stretch my money longer, diversify my wardrobe, and made me a better seamstress. One caveat about thrifting is that now that it has gotten so main stream there are a lot more stores where the sorting through everything is already done for you. It is then marked up at a higher price than what they bought it for at places like Goodwill, Deseret Industries etc. That's my soapbox that I could stand on for hours but I won't subject anyone to that torture.
